UX Design Projects

A selection of UX design projects where I explored user needs through research, prototyping, and testing to create intuitive and accessible digital experiences.

Digital Nomad

Digital Nomad is a mobile app concept designed to help freelancers gain visibility and create new opportunities by showcasing their work and sharing their skills. The app includes a portfolio page where freelancers can present their projects and a learning hub where they can offer courses or live sessions in areas such as marketing, design, and web development. Through research and user testing, I explored challenges freelancers face, such as income instability and lack of visibility.

  • Figma
Live Prototype Figma Design

Airbnb

This project focused on improving the Airbnb website experience by optimizing navigation and the booking system. Through usability testing, I analyzed how users interact with the platform and identified several friction points. The research showed confusion around the budget filter, low visibility of filters, and unclear information on the homepage. Based on these insights, I redesigned the filters, integrated them into the search bar, and improved typography to enhance clarity and accessibility.

  • Figma
Live Prototype Figma Design

Okalpha

This project involved designing a high-fidelity contact form page for the design company Okalpha, which specializes in web development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing. The goal was to create a visually appealing and accessible contact experience for all users. I designed an interactive prototype focusing on usability, intuitive navigation, and accessibility guidelines. The process included research on accessible forms, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing to refine the experience and ensure a smooth and inclusive interaction.
